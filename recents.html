Ranking of Shows I've Watched Recently

Click Names for a Link to Trailer
Number Name Review
5 Cowboy Bebop Very fun to watch. I really have no complaints about this show. Ranked low because I haven't finished it.
4 Jujutsu Kaisen Definitely one of the best new animes to come out in the past year or so. This was definitely in the number three spot before I watched the last few episodes. Not a fan of how season one was concluded.
3 Demon Slayer Demon Slayer was very slow at first, but after the movie came out I'm actually excited to see where the story goes. Very excited to see what happens next.
2 Jojo's Bizarre Adventure If you've seen Jojo's there's absoulutely no surprise here. One of if not the best pieces of fiction to ever be created and the anime will only get better as more is released.
1 Mob Psycho 100 A hard choice between this and Jojo's for number one. Ultimately, I like a lot of the characters more in this and the development of those characters is unrivaled by really anything else I've seen. Season 3 was recently announced and I can't wait to see what happens next.
